It's In The Word with Cynthia Marie Duncan


Giving Up

 

(6 Min 45 Sec)

There are times when you just want to throw in the towel! "Just forget it, I don't want any part with ... I just want to quit!" Ever been there? If you have, listen in to learn how to overcome and keep on going.


Favor

 

(7 Min 29 Sec)

When you have the favor of the Lord working in your life, God will bring you through with life and power to victory.


Focus

 

(8 Min 5 Sec)

Staying focused in ministry can be very difficult when you have so many other things pulling at you. Are you in focus or in the upheavel of a war zone? Listen in and you too will learn how to stay focused.


Infirmities

 

( 6 Min 40 Sec)

What takes us so long to finally let go and let God heal us of our infirmities? Why do we tend to hold on to some of the unhealthy things in our lives? Listen in to let go and let God.


Leaving things in the Hands of the Lord

 

(6 Min 29 Sec)

It seems that everyone I come across lately is struggling with something or another. They try to handle it themselves, but the best place to put it all is in the Hands of the Lord. Listen in as Cynthia encourages you to put it in His hands.
 
 


Love

 

(9 Min 47 Sec)

We all have what we call our love language; so when we talk about "love" it may not mean the same thing to you as it does to the next person.

So what is love?


Making A Fresh Start

 

(8 Minutes 25 Seconds)

Are you tired of the same old rut? Do you need to breakout and start something new? Listen in as Cynthia Marie Duncan teaches you how to make a fresh start!
 


Real Worship

 

(7 Min 52 Sec)

What is worship; how do we worship God? What is real worship? What is a false sense of worship? Is God pleased with my worship? If you've asked yourself any of these questions, stay tuned as Cynthia shares her heart of worship.
