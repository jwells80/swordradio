It's In The Word with Cynthia Marie Duncan


Sacrifice

 

(5 Min 40 Sec)

There have been many times in my life that I have had to sacrifice something that wasn't easy. Sometimes we can give up some things that aren't that hard to do, but what about those sacrifices that really hurt and are difficult? Are willing to give up some of the things you love the most for God?


Self-Rejection

 

(8 Min 50 Sec)

I read someplace that many people struggle with a poor self image and find it hard to accept themselves. Often, the contributing thought patterns have exisited for years. So how then can the cycle be broken? Stay tuned to find out.


Silence

 

(7 Min 48 Sec)

Being silent is one of the hardest things to do for a lot of people. There are times when silence is very important in order for us to hear what God is trying to tell us.


Stepping Out

 

( 7 Min 10 Sec)

God has given each of us some type of talent or gift and a lot of times we are afraid to step out and get our feet wet and see what God will do with us. Get ready to step out and take that leap of faith to find out what God really wants you to do!


Wholeness

 

(6 Min 45 Sec)
 
A lot of people are in search of wholeness in their lives because they feel incomplete. They try to find their completeness in others while all along their completeness and wholeness is in Jesus Christ.
