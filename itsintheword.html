It's In The Word

Cynthia Marie Duncan

Cynthia Marie Duncan was born in Springfield, Missouri where she currently resides with her husband Arthur.

An ordained minister, psalmist, speaker, and teacher, she also hosts “It's In The Word,” an internet radio broadcast. “Everything you need to lead a victorious life is `In The Word,'” says Cynthia.

In 2005, together with her husband and friends, Cynthia formed a gospel singing group called “Higher Praise.” If you would like information about “Higher Praise,” or would like to reach Cynthia, please contact her through e-mail duncan2@peoplepc.com, or (417) 693-8375.
