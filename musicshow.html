Le`Beat

Buffy St. John

Declare The Word International's founder and president, Buffy St. John has a heart for God and equipping those called by God to reach their destiny. Coming from a music background both in the secular and christian world Buffy's vision to reach the youth of today is coming to fruition as she launches her new show called Le` Beat. This is a platform in which selected bands, solo artists and performers will gain exposure for their music ministry as well as be equipped to do the things that will launch them forth either as an indie or signed artist.

Buffy's heart is to teach others to be all that they can be with no strings attached but for the joy of seeing God's hand at work in other's lives. On the flip side you all get to listen to music and the up and coming artists that God created. How cool is that? So Enjoy! And let us hear from you!
