How Do I...

 

I've received a number of calls, requesting I train them to create their own radio show, or podcast. We believe in equipping people and sending them out to accomplish their destiny. A phone call helps one person, but putting it out on the web helps innumerable people. With that said, I trust that the following will help you be creative, share the love of Christ and spread His Word. God Bless You!

First you'll need a computer with audio speakers and a computer microphone (you can pick up a mic at most stores including Wal-Mart) You'll also need  an audio recording and editing software. There are many out there, but for the price (FREE), I use Audacity. You can download it at http://audacity.sourceforge.net

I would recommend that you download the software manual and print it out. Trust me. It comes in handy! Download at: http://audacity.sourceforge.net/help/documentation

Now that you've gotten that done,  I suggest you follow the link below.They are great people and offer free training! Best of all they have a 30 Day FREE TRIAL!!!
